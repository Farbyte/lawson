## What is Lawson ?

Lawson is a tool to help lawyers understand legal documents. It is a web-based tool that uses machine learning to extract key information from legal documents and present it in a user-friendly way. Lawson can help lawyers quickly identify key clauses, provisions, and other important information in legal documents, saving them time and effort.

## How does Lawson work ?

Lawson uses natural language processing and machine learning techniques to analyze legal documents. It can extract key information such as parties, dates, obligations, and other important details from legal documents. Lawson then presents this information in a user-friendly format, making it easy for lawyers to review and understand the document.
