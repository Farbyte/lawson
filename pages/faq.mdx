# FAQ

## Why is Lawson useful ?

Lawson is useful because it is a tool that can be used to generate summaries of legal documents. It can also be used to chat with the legal documents to easily get the important information.

## Why was it built ?

It was built mainly for the purpose of participating in the Hashnode [AI for tommorow hackathon](https://hashnode.com/hackathons/ai-for-tomorrow).
